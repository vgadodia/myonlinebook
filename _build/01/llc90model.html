LLC90 Model

The LLC90 model uses the ECCOv4r3 physical model to provide physical circulation and hydrographic fields. The physical fields are generated by assimilating observations for the period 1992 - 2017 into a global ocean configuration of the MIT General Circulation Model (MITgcm).
